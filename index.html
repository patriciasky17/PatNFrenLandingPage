Your relationship with child matters...

MemoTales emerged from a passion for enhancing parent-child relationships and promoting childhood development. Recognizing the immense influence of parent-child interactions, we crafted an app for interactive storytelling to educate, entertain and foster relationships.

Our app, designed with care and expertise, offers engaging characters, storylines, and mini-games for collaborative parent-child adventures, with the goal of seeing families worldwide share smiles, laughter, and learning.
